TTC Internal Tools

Joseph's private dashboards for running Total Tree Care. One home for all internal links. Not linked from any customer- or crew-facing app.

Internal use. These pages have public web addresses but are not advertised anywhere; treat the links as private. Each tool reads live data from its Google Sheet on open.

Chip Drop QA dashboard →

Eyeball every non-active chip-drop pin against its submitted address before you set it Active. Pulls the live v2 sheet on open.

PHC Calendar →

The biology clock: live growing degree days (Logan) + every open PHC job classified PAST / CLOSING / OPEN / DORMANT against its treatment window. Refreshed by the daily phc-scan.
